Technology can be termed as the application of scientific knowledge for practical purpose. Every machine today that we are surrounded by is an example of technology. It is the tools, methods, and systems that are developed and used to improve and simplify daily life. This includes the creation and use of devices, software, and systems in a variety of fields including information technology, transportation, healthcare, communication, and many more. Technology is developed by humans to improve and simplify their lives, as well as to advance knowledge and understanding in various fields. It is constantly evolving and improving, leading to new innovations and advancements in various industries. With the rapid growth of technological advancement in recent years, technology has become increasingly important for both individuals and society. Technology now has become an integral part of our livelihood, which has made our lives easier and efficient. Technology has completely changed our lives to significant extent. Things we cannot imagine a few years ago, has become a part of our lives. Technology has played a significant role in shaping the modern world and has brought numerous benefits, including improved communication, increased efficiency and productivity, and access to information and resources.
Technology has both positive and negative effect in modern society. Some of the positive and negative impacts of technology are described below:

Positive Impact:
There are various positive impacts of technology, they include the followings:
1. Improved quality of life: Technology has improved many aspects of daily life. It has made the life of people more enjoyable.
2. Improved education: Technology has made education more accessible and interactive, allowing students to learn in new and innovative ways.
3. Time management: Technology helps in managing time by contributing in saving significant time of people.
4. Enhanced entertainment: Technology has brought new forms of entertainment, such as video games, streaming services, and social media that has changed the way people spend their leisure time.
5. Increased efficiency and productivity: Technology has made many tasks easier, faster and more efficient, leading to increased productivity in various fields.
6. Increased connectivity: Technology has made it easier for people to connect and collaborate with each other and make a good connection among themselves.
7. Better healthcare: Medical technology has advanced to the point where doctors can treat patients more effectively, leading to improved health outcomes.
8. Better access to information: The internet has vast amount of information so, Technology has made it easier for people to access information and knowledge through the internet, online libraries, and databases.

Negative Impacts:
There are various negative impacts of technology, they include the followings:
1. Cyber Security risks: The increasing reliance on technology may led to various risks such as Cyber Attack, Data Breaches, and many other forms of online exploitation and theft.
2. Physical health concerns: People spend excessive amount of time on the technology which may affect peoples physical health which can have negative impact on them.
3. Mental health concerns: Spending excessive time on technology has been linked to a range of mental health issues, including stress, anxiety, and depression.
4. Addiction: Some people may develop addictive relationships with technology, spending excessive amounts of time on their devices and neglecting other important aspects of their lives. It may lead them to loneliness and social isolation.
5. Cyberbullying and harassment: The anonymity of the internet has led to an increase in cyberbullying and online harassment.
6. Disinformation and misinformation: All the information provided online are not all true. The ease of spreading information online has led to the spread of false or misleading information, known as "fake news.‚Äù
7. Privacy concerns: The increased use of technology has raised concerns about privacy, as personal information is often collected and shared by companies.
8. Environmental impact: The production and disposal of electronic devices have a negative impact on the environment, including e-waste and toxic pollution.

The impact of technology on selling bonsai plants can be both positive and negative. They are mentioned below:
Positive impacts:
1. Increased visibility and accessibility: Technology has made it easier for bonsai sellers to reach a wider audience and make their products more accessible to potential customers.
2. Improved customer experience: Online platforms allow customers to easily browse and purchase bonsai plants, making the buying process more convenient and user-friendly.
3. Access to information and resources: Technology provides bonsai sellers with access to a wealth of information and resources that can help them improve their business and reach customers more effectively.

5. Wider reach: Technology enables bonsai plant sellers to reach a wider audience, including those who live in remote or hard-to-reach areas.

Negative impacts:

3. Shipping and handling: Shipping live plants can be challenging and requires special care, which can negatively impact the customer experience if not handled properly.
